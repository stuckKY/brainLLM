#### How nationalism works with other ideologies
- Elements of nationalism can contribute to other ideologies, giving them context and legitimacy
- Can also be used to enhance the persuasiveness of other ideologies by being associated with a supposed national character or sentiment
- Is complex and can combine with other ideologies to forge a political belief system that seems coherent and natural
- E.g. often seen as antagonistic with liberalism, but
	- JS Mill recognised importance of a sense of nationality in sustaining claims about rights and legal equalities
	- Contemporary liberal political theorists are beginning to see ways in which a sense of nationality may be important
		- Particularly true for communitarianism - which argues that liberalism is insufficiently attentive to the need of individuals to be part of a wider substantive community
